WHy you shouldn't use Windows

Reasons you should not choose Windows as your computer's OS...

There are a number of reasons you should not use Windows.

  • Privacy concerns. People have said that Microsoft collects data about your computer. This is most likely true. Someone has also caught Microsoft sending data, even though they DISABLED it specifically when they set it up.
  • It's the most vulnerable to viruses. Using the most popular OS comes with a cost- since many people use the OS, hackers are more likely to target that OS. Targeting can be done with ads or fishy or suspicious links.
  • It crashes or freezes sometimes. Windows is often unstable on some computers. It uses a lot of ram for a start, more than 2GB on a 8GB ram computer. In addition, It also runs too many things in the background. Even the "Your Phone" app runs in the background when you haven't opened it!
